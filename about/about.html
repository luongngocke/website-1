Who is this man? Dude, myth or a Developer

Rohan is a freelance python app and web developer. He build some awesome python games which are hosted in heroku and github. He is also a very good in Linus System Administration. He is working in linux for 4 years long and have some brilliant ideas.

That´s enough of talking about myself in the third person.

At first I intended to be an engineer and went to a B.Tech school fully motivated to become just that. One thing led to another and 3 years went by and I was a (almost) fully fledged freelance python developer without ever planning to become one.

This fortunate turn of events has lead me to develop python games in codeskulptor GalaxyInvanders, Pong,RiceRocks. To work with people from all over the world is very rewarding. Sweden, Denmark, Iceland, Germany, India, USA and England are some of the countries I have worked with. Well not the country itself, but people living there courtesy to coursers.org.

I have coded websites with great respect to standards, because it makes life easier for everybody. My site even used to validate, honestly!

I live in India and work both in My home and My College as well as traveling to my friend's home to work very closely with them there. This can be anywhere on this Planet, still waiting for warp technology to develop to go beyond it.
